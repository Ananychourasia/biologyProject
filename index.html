Biodiversity

Biodiversity means the variability among different living organisms.We can also recognise biodiversity by different species.

Biodiversity is shortened from two words - biological diversity

Best example of biodiversity are plants and animals

Why do we need biodiversity

  • Biodiversity offers us food
  • Biodiversity is fundamentally important for our medicines
  • It supports us ecologically and livelihood of many people involved in farming are supported by biodiversity
  • Economics of some country depend upon biodiversity
  • Biodiversity scientifically explains us, how the world works
